Xcelerate - Fueling Ideas. Accelerating Dreams

Xcelerate is a Shark Tank-style schedule designed to bring together venture capitalists (VCs), angel investors, and startups led by students and professionals. This dynamic platform provides an opportunity for budding entrepreneurs to pitch their innovative ideas, seek mentorship, and secure potential investments from seasoned investors. The schedule fosters collaboration, inspires creativity, and promotes entrepreneurial spirit among participants.

Schedule
  • Round 1: Submission of Pitch Deck (via Unstop)
  • Participants must submit a detailed pitch deck of their idea in the form of a PowerPoint presentation (PPT). Submitting a combination of a PPT and a video explaining the concept is optional but encouraged for a better understanding of the idea.

    Start: 27 Jan 25, 03:30 PM IST
    End: 10 Feb 25, 12:00 PM IST

  • Round 2: Live Pitch at E-Summit
  • Selected teams will present their ideas at XIM University’s E-Summit, gaining exposure to investors, experts, and peers.

    Start: 16 Feb 25, 09:00 AM IST
    End: 17 Feb 25, 10:00 PM IST

Prizes&

Perks
  • Funding opportunities from VCs and angel investors
  • Exclusive 1:1 mentorship sessions with seasoned entrepreneurs and investors
  • Certificates of participation for all shortlisted teams
Eligibility

This schedule is open to students, professionals, and aspiring entrepreneurs from diverse backgrounds who aim to make an impact with their innovative ideas.

Participants will engage in rounds that test their critical thinking, creativity, and ability to design compelling marketing strategies for brands. It would be followed by a short pitch where the participants will get a chance to present their product and marketing strategy.

  • Participants will receive a brand approximately 72 hours before the submission deadline. Teams are required to create and submit a detailed marketing strategy, including visual/creative elements (posters, videos, etc.) along with a brief description of their approach. Submission Format: PDF (Max 5 MB)

    Hints and Tips: Follow ecell_ximuniversity on Instagram for exclusive hints to aid your strategy creation!

Perks

Turnaround Titans - The Business Revival Challenge

A competition where participants will analyze real world business scenarios and propose innovative solutions. This schedule aims to test critical thinking, problem-solving, and presentation skills. Teams will tackle complex case studies, presenting their insights and strategies to a panel of judges comprising industry experts and faculty members.

Stages & Timeline
  • Round 1: Preliminary Round - Case Analysis
  • In the first round, teams will analyze a case scenario involving a struggling company. Participants are required to identify the company’s critical challenges and propose high-level revival strategies. Submissions will include a 2-3 slide presentation and a one-page executive summary that clearly outlines the problems and proposed solutions. Teams will be evaluated based on the depth of problem identification, creativity, feasibility of solutions, and the clarity of their submissions. The top-performing teams will advance to the final round.

    Start: 01 Feb 25, 12:00 PM ISTT
    End: 04 Feb 25, 11:59 PM IST

  • Final Round – Revival Plan and Live Pitch
  • In the final round, shortlisted teams will develop a detailed revival plan addressing operational, financial, and marketing strategies for the case provided. Teams will prepare an 8-10 slide presentation to outline their strategies and pitch their plan live to a panel of judges. The presentation will be followed by a Q&A session, where teams will defend their ideas and respond to questions from the judges. The final evaluation will focus on the innovation and practicality of the revival plan, the quality of the presentation, and the team’s ability to handle the Q&A effectively. Winners will be announced based on their overall performance in this round.

    Start: 16 Feb 25, 09:00 AM IST
    End: 17 Feb 25, 10:30 PM IST

Guidelines

  • External resources are permitted, provided they are properly cited.
  • Professionalism is expected throughout the competition.
Eligibility

Open to students and professionals from any domain who are ready to apply teamwork, analytical thinking, and problem-solving skills.
